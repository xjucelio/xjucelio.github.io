His Story

Bruno was a calm, independent, and affectionate tuxedo cat—one of those rare souls who bring serenity simply by being near.

Bruno was Maria’s beloved cat—a beautiful black-and-white boy with a glossy coat, white chest and paws, and golden eyes full of quiet curiosity.

He carried himself with remarkable calm. He could fall into a deep, peaceful sleep in any warm corner—on the sofa, tucked away somewhere cozy, or high above the room after climbing up to observe the world.

Independent, yet deeply affectionate, he gave gentle headbutts, welcomed a warm lap when he felt like it, and filled the house with that special kind of presence that turns a place into a home.
