His Story

Bruno was Maria’s beloved cat — a beautiful black-and-white tuxedo with a glossy coat, white chest and paws, and golden eyes full of quiet curiosity.

He had a remarkably smooth and tranquil personality. He could fall into a deep sleep in any warm corner — on the sofa, tucked away, or up high after climbing to observe the world.

Independent, yet deeply affectionate: gentle headbutts, warm laps when he chose, and that calm energy that truly makes a house feel like home.
